Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

This page explains what information Civics Test Practice ("this site," "we," "us") collects and how it's used. This site is a free, independently run study tool and is not affiliated with USCIS or the U.S. government.

Information you provide directly

This site does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use. Answers you type into the Quiz view stay in your browser only — they are never sent anywhere or saved. Bookmarked questions are also kept only in your browser's temporary memory and are cleared when you close or refresh the page.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns, such as how many people visit, which pages are popular, and what device or browser they're using. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects information such as your approximate location (city/region level), device type, and how you interact with the site. This data is aggregated and is not used to personally identify you. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at Google's Privacy & Terms page, and you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising

This site does not currently show ads. If ads are added in the future (for example, through Google AdSense), this policy will be updated, and third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You would be able to opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party links

This site links to external sites such as uscis.gov and GitHub. We aren't responsible for the privacy practices of those sites — please review their own privacy policies.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for general audiences studying for the U.S. naturalization test and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time, for example if ads or new features are added. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Visit the Contact page.
