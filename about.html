About This Site

Last updated July 2026

Civics Test Practice is a free study tool built for people preparing for the civics portion of the U.S. naturalization interview. It was created by a solo hobbyist project — not a company, not a test-prep business — with one goal: make it easier and less stressful to study the 128 official questions.

Where the content comes from

Every question and answer on this site is taken directly from USCIS Form M-1778 (09/25), the official 2025 version of the civics test, published by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services. This site is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to USCIS or any part of the U.S. government.

How to use it

The Flashcard view lets you flip through questions one at a time at your own pace. The Quiz view lets you type your own answer before revealing the official one, so you can honestly check what you actually know. You can filter by category, focus on just the 20 questions used for the 65/20 special consideration test, and bookmark questions you want to revisit.

Why it's free

Studying for a citizenship test is stressful enough without paywalls. This site will always be free to use. It may eventually show ads to cover hosting costs, but the core study tool itself will never be locked behind a paywall.
