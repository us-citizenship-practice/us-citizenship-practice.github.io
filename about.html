About This Site

Last updated July 2026

Civics Test Practice is a free, interactive study tool designed for anyone preparing for the civics portion of the U.S. naturalization interview. It was created as a solo hobbyist project — not by a company or a commercial test-prep business — with one simple goal: to make it easier, clearer, and less stressful to study the official citizenship questions.

Where the content comes from

To ensure absolute accuracy, every question and answer on this site is sourced directly from official government materials:

  • 2025 Test Version: Sourced from USCIS 128 Civics Questions and Answers.
  • 2008 Test Version: Sourced from USCIS 100 Civics Questions and Answers (English).

Please note that this site is an independent, non-profit project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) or any branch of the U.S. government.

How to use it

Our tool automatically guides you through a quick onboarding wizard to determine which test version applies to you based on your N-400 filing date and age. Once loaded, you can choose between two modes:

  • The Flashcard view lets you flip through questions one at a time to build active recall at your own pace.
  • The Quiz view challenges you to type out your answers before revealing the official key, allowing you to honestly evaluate your preparation.

You can also narrow your focus by category, study the shortened 20-question list if you qualify for the "65/20" special exemption, and bookmark particularly tricky questions to review later.

Why it's free

Studying for a citizenship test is stressful enough without paywalls. This site will always be free to use. It may eventually show ads to cover hosting costs, but the core study tool itself will never be locked behind a paywall.
